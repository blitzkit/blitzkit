# BlitzKit 1.29.13

## Features

- The screenshot button now automatically crops your image so you don't have excessive blank space all around
- Increased the max zoom out distance in Tankopedia to 50m

## Fixes

- Turrets in Tankopedia now stay within a range of -180 and +180 degrees
- Tanks with transparency like the T34 and Herdbreaker are now properly rendered
  - This will take a few days to roll out for all tanks (see screenshots below for the fixed models)
- High End Consumables now give a 33% boost instead of 70%
- Static Armor now properly deselects all irrelevant armor plates
- Removed a few debug loggers left behind
- Static Armor now properly rerenders armor plates when their thicknesses are changed
- The Ricochet Visualizer now stays in place when dragging around on mobile
- Reduced the text in the Tankopedia Compare segmented display to fit on smaller screens
- Sorting by tertiary shells no longer crashes for some tanks
- The Tank Filters are now left aligned on smaller screens for readability
