# Christmas Garage Guide 2025

The holiday season has arrived, and this year, the celebrations take over your garage with more than just festive décor.

![](https://i.imgur.com/HmbDw0x.jpeg)

## Win Battles, Earn Rewards

Every victory earns you **3 Hot Coffees**, which you can trade in for Festive Town rewards.

![](https://i.imgur.com/MXGxejz.jpeg)

You’ll also be able to claim **2 tank certificates**, one for **tier VII** and one for **tier IX–X** machines. Possible prizes include:

**Tier VII Certificate Tanks**: [E 25](https://blitzkit.app/tanks/e-25), [CS-52 LIS](https://blitzkit.app/tanks/cs-52-lis), [AMX 13 57](https://blitzkit.app/tanks/amx-13-57), [Steyr WT](https://blitzkit.app/tanks/steyr-wt), [Kunze Panzer](https://blitzkit.app/tanks/kunze-panzer), [Super Hellcat](https://blitzkit.app/tanks/super-hellcat)

**Tier IX–X Certificate Tanks**: [121B](https://blitzkit.app/tanks/121b), [AMX 30 B](https://blitzkit.app/tanks/amx-30-b), [Obj. 260](https://blitzkit.app/tanks/obj-260), [Char Futur 4](https://blitzkit.app/tanks/char-futur-4), [TL-7-120](https://blitzkit.app/tanks/tl-7-120), [Object 452K](https://blitzkit.app/tanks/object-452k)

![](https://i.imgur.com/lxr7PE9.png)

## Upgrade Your Christmas Tree

Golden tokens let you raise your tree through **four upgrade stages**. A fully upgraded tree grants up to **+25% silver** per battle.

![](https://i.imgur.com/bcmQE8T.jpeg)

You’ll also unlock ornament slots, eight in total, on the left-hand side of your screen.

![](https://i.imgur.com/MMbyYLn.jpeg)

## Upgrade Your Yuletide Stall

Your golden tokens can also enhance the **Yuletide Stall**, unlocking rewards at every level.

![](https://i.imgur.com/AONZN5O.jpeg)

Here’s how it looks before and after upgrading:

![](https://i.imgur.com/8fNy0Yj.png)

## Upgrade Your Shooting Gallery

Another area to enhance is the **Shooting Gallery**. Upgrading it unlocks new rewards and increases your options for exchanging mastery badges.

![](https://i.imgur.com/Rj1WNCE.jpeg)

This is also where you’ll find this year’s Snow Globe replacement, the **Music Box**.

![](https://i.imgur.com/GAUd2uR.png)

## The Music Box

Out with Snow Globes, in with **Music Boxes**.

![](https://i.imgur.com/sxYV77L.jpeg)

These offer a chance to earn the brand-new **[GSOR the TANK](https://blitzkit.app/tanks/gsor-the-tank/)** at no cost.

![](https://i.imgur.com/pGO9lIg.png)

This year’s Christmas Garage brings a full mix of festive ambience, rewarding progression, and chances at powerful tanks, all wrapped in a set of activities that keep every battle meaningful. Upgrade your locations, collect tokens, and try your luck with Music Boxes to make the most of the event. Dive in, enjoy the celebrations, and make this holiday season your most profitable yet on the battlefield.
